5x5 Protocol

Strength • Prehab • Form

Workout A

A Days
Barbell Squats
5 × 5 Lower Body

Rest barbell across upper back. Squat until thighs are parallel to the floor, drive up through mid-foot.

Bench Press
5 × 5 Chest / Triceps

Lie flat, feet planted. Lower bar to mid-chest, pause, and press to full extension.

Barbell Rows
5 × 5 Back

Hinge forward at hips. Pull bar to lower ribs, squeezing shoulder blades together.

Poliquin Step-Ups
3 × 12 Knee Prehab

Stand on a low step. Keep working leg straight, slowly lower other heel to tap floor.

Lateral Raises
3 × 15 Side Delts

Slight bend in elbows, raise arms out to sides until shoulder height.

Workout B

B Days
Barbell Squats
5 × 5 Lower Body

Rest barbell across upper back. Squat until thighs are parallel to the floor, drive up through mid-foot.

Overhead Press
5 × 5 Front Delts / Triceps

Brace core, press bar straight up overhead, lock elbows at top.

Deadlifts
1 × 5 Posterior Chain

Hinge to grip bar, keep back flat, stand by pushing floor away.

Lying Leg Curls
3 × 12 Knee Prehab

Curl heels toward glutes to strengthen hamstrings.

Cable Face Pulls
3 × 15 Rear Delts / Posture

Pull rope toward face, flaring elbows high. Squeeze upper back.
